Item Arrival


  • Inventory management> journal Entries >Item Arrival

  • Click new
  • Fill the packing slip with vendor provided invoice.

  • Fill warehouse, Site, Account number, PO number and click OK.

  • Click function and create lines.
  • Add manufacturer date, Expiry date, Batch number.

  • Users need to Click on Validate Button and Than Post Button.

  • If the item is damaged,wastage or short received kindly follow below steps.
    •
  • Click functions in journal lines.
  • Click split and Mention the item qty in split.
  • click ok
  • In line details click dimension and select location.
  • Select the wastage or short and damaged location the qty.

  • Click Validate and Post.
  • To check GRN go to purchase order tab.
  • Users need to click on Receive tab after that Product Receipt Tab in all Purchase order
  • Check the product receipt journal
